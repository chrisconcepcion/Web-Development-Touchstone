Our Story

Welcome to Bloom Valley Nursery, where our passion for plants meets personalized service!

Started in 2020 by a family of garden enthusiasts, we've grown from a small backyard operation into your local destination for all things green. Our team doesn't just sell plants we eat them just like true vegans would.

Whether you're a first-time plant parent or a seasoned gardener, we're here to share our knowledge and help your garden thrive. Every plant in our nursery is hand-selected and cared for with attention to detail, ensuring you get the healthiest starts for your space.
